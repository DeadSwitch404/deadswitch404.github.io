New Release: Whisper Engine v1.1.0


The Whisper Engine moves without noise.
But today, it speaks.

Version v1.1.0 is released.
A new capability emerges: RSS feed generation.

The engine now writes a clean and minimal rss.xml file containing the latest ten posts.

Every entry carries its title, canonical URL, teaser, and date.
No tracking. No analytics. Only the signal.

The output validates under xmllint without a single warning.
Readers and aggregators can follow the whispers without touching the rest of the site.

This is the new layer of the GhostWare stack:
silent publication, machine-readable presence.
