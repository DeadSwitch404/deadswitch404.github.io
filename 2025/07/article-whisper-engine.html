Known Limitations of the Whisper

Silence has its price. Minimalism cuts both ways.
The Whisper Engine is simple, self-contained, dedicated to Emacs users.

A full workflow achievable from an Emacs frame.

Yet, there are some warnings ahead:

  • Full purge before deploy. No ghosts left behind.
  • Header and footer elements are embedded into the lisp code ( plans for external DOM templates)
  • 'id' attributes are stripped away entirely from the HTML (temporary fix for another problem)
  • No RSS (yet)
  • No site map (yet)
  • No themes (only CSS)
  • Custom static pages are hardcoded (for now)

Despite these, the Whisper Engine is functional. Solid. Extensible.
