Tools - Your Digital Fortress

Your mind is alert. Your focus is sharp.
Your tools strengthen the shield.
They cover your attack surface.

Choose them wisely.

  • Your Hardware is encrypted.
    Set a strong BIOS/EFI password.
    Don't let anyone touch your hardware.

    Avoid noisy brands.
    Old ThinkPads are a great budget choice.

  • Your Linux is your castle - your operations center.

    LUKS-encrypted drives and filesystems.
    Strong, complex keys.

    Password-protected bootloader.
    No " safe boot". No init=/bin/sh.
    Harden the kernel. Lock the network. Clean the process tree.

    Change your MAC for every new connection.
    Your physical trace fades.

    Use a general Linux distro. Debian is solid.

    DeadSwitch Whisper

    Linux is your Digital Heart. Keep it trained & healthy.

    Don't sign in to the cloud - it's loud.
