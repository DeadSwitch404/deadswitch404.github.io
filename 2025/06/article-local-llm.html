LMStudio

LMStudio is a local AI launcher.
Simple. Effective.
It can run Llama.cpp, Mistral, or other models without leaving the machine.

Tactical Setup:

  • Load small to medium models for fast iterations.
  • Keep it air-gapped if possible.
  • Run without telemetry. Strip the noise.

LMStudio is a good first blade for local AI.
